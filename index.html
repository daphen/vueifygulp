Space Ipsum

What was most significant about the lunar voyage was not that man set foot on the Moon but that they set eye on the earth. We are all connected; To each other, biologically. To the earth, chemically. To the rest of the universe atomically.

Curious that we spend more time congratulating people who have succeeded than encouraging people who have not. If you could see the earth illuminated when you were in a place as dark as night, it would look to you more splendid than the moon.
